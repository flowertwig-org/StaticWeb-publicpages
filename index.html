Getting started with StaticWeb!

How to get started using StaticWeb in 3 steps. read more

Step 1 - Sign in

Click the "Sign in" link at the top and choose a storage provider that best sutes you. We will store everything you create and all settings you change on this provider, none of it will be stored on our end.

Step 2 - Content and layout

There are two ways to add content and layouts to StaticWeb

Step 2A - Start from scratch

Upload or create layout templates and then create content

Step 2B - Clone existing website

Enter the adress to your website and then press "Clone". StaticWeb will now crawl your website and collect content and layouts for you to approve later.

Step 3 - Publish website

Publish changes on your website
